Hello, I'm Matthew

Hello, my name is Matthew Moore and I am a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing the real, the raw, and the unforgettable. Based in Pewaukee, Wisconsin, I've been pursuing photography professionally since 2023, focusing on what matters most—the pride in a senior's eyes as they step into their final year, the adrenaline of a game-winning moment, or the joy of walking across the graduation stage—I'm there for it all.

I specialize in senior portraits, sports photography, event coverage, and graduation sessions, bringing a creative edge and a calm, professional vibe to every shoot. My goal is to not just take photos, but to tell your story—whether you're an athlete in motion, a student celebrating a milestone, or a proud parent cheering from the crowd.

Every session is personalized, relaxed, and designed to highlight what makes you stand out. I believe the best photos come when you're being yourself, and I'm here to help you feel confident, comfortable, and seen.

Let's create something memorable together—photos that not only look good, but feel like you.

My Approach

Photography isn't just about clicking a button—it's about capturing real emotion, real moments, and real people.

My approach is centered around connection, authenticity, and storytelling. Whether I'm photographing a senior ready to take on the world, an athlete in their element, or a graduate celebrating the end of an incredible journey, I focus on creating a space where people feel comfortable, confident, and truly themselves.

Before every session, I take time to understand what makes you unique—your style, your energy, your story. During the shoot, I keep things relaxed and natural so nothing feels forced or stiff. I use a mix of guided direction and candid moments to bring out the best version of you on camera.

For sports, I look for intensity, motion, and those split-second expressions that tell the whole story of a game. For events, I focus on capturing the atmosphere, emotion, and interactions that make every occasion special. For seniors and grads, I aim to freeze a moment in time that reflects both who you are and who you're becoming.

At the end of the day, I'm not just here to take great photos—I'm here to help you feel seen, celebrated, and proud of where you are right now.
